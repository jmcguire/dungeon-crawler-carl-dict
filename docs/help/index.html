Help

Need help with the dictionary?

Did you notice anything wrong in the dictionary, something missing, or something that does not work? Or do you just need help?

Open the help form

What to include

You do not need to know any technical language. Just describe what happened and what you were trying to do.

  • Your device or app, such as Kindle, Kobo, KOReader, or BOOX.
  • Which dictionary file you downloaded, if you know.
  • The entry, phrase, or word involved, if something in the dictionary seems missing or wrong.
  • What you expected to happen and what actually happened.
  • If you need help, just tell me what you need
